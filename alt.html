About

alt. is a location based application that is dedicated to bringing back small buisnesses and helping promote local economies grow.This site will sell anything that can be bought and mailed to the customer. Products can include food, crafts, homemade items, clothes, electronics, furniture, etc.

Project Duration

3 weeks

My Process

Development - 5 Ws

Who

Anyone. Anyone that is looking to bring back small buisness and are into high quality products.

What

A responsive web app to give people a easy way to navigate on all platforms.

Where

Anywhere that there is internet access. Most users will probably use at home.

Why

People are looking to support local smaller buisness now more than ever. People like to shop online to save time during their busy work schedule and may not have access to some products in there local area.

Final Screen Designs

Custom Home Screen Based on Location

Home screen will feature local buisnesses and products that you can search for. You can adjust your location and order all around the country for small buisnesses as well.

Mission Statement

The home page explains the website's mission on helping small buisnesses grow after the negative impact of the COVID pandemic. A donate button is featured on the home screen for users to donate towards building strong local communities

Product Page

Product page offers user to add to cart and continue shopping and it also enables the user to have a barter offer using an alternative currency for people limited on money.

Retrospective

Throughout the process of creating this application I tried my hardest to make this different than other competitior apps like Etsy and Amazon. Eventhough this is a ecommerce application it is also a community building application so it is very important to show users how the application is giving back and helping the community grow.

The main lesson I learned throughout this design process was how to make an already saturated market of apps stand out and how to include different functionality that other apps do not have.
