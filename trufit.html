Explorer

About

Explorer is a responsive location based web application that helps the user plan the perfect trip anywhere around the world. The user can discover new travel destinations with ease and always have something new to do not matter where they are.

Tools Used

Project Duration

6 weeks

My Process

User Flow

I developed my user flow based on what my 5 Ws were and what functionality was essential to be incorporated within the application.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

Mid Fidelity

After I developed my low fidelity wireframes, I created my mid fidelity version   to get the layout coherent and added more detail to get a better idea of what the final design was going to look like.

User Prototyping 

After finalizing the layout and design of the Trufit application I had 5 users test my IOS and Android version to help finalize the design. I selected my user based on what I thought my target market would be. My target market would be for anyone looking to improve their overall health and develop a new fitness lifestyle.

IOS - Dashboard

Android - Login/Sign Up

User Prototyping Conclusion

Overall I received some great insights on what I needed to include in my application such as graphic adjustments and overall functionalities such as an in depth questionnaire to help the user know how they’re personalized dashboard is being created..

Final Screen Designs

Retrospective

After going through this process of developing my fitness app for both IOS and Android platforms I have learned a lot about design and functionality. Some missing screens that weren’t developed during the UX phase of the design process left a couple users confused when testing the product. It’s important to make the design to be able translate to either platform so that the users will always get a great experience.
